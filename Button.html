BENTE

Push the button to escape

Push The Button to Escape is a work I created in 2023 after a project week led by Tim Carson. I developed a tool that generates coordinates of places where you cannot be seen by surveillance cameras, based on publicly available information about camera locations.

The idea was that each time you press the button, you briefly “escape” society. At the same time, the work raises questions about how constantly we are being watched. The map of Antwerp with all camera positions helps make this visible.

When you push the button, you get the coordinates from a place without camera's.

The work was shown at Antwerp Art 2023.
