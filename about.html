About me

I’m Bente Govaerts (2003°), and I am a Jewellery Design student at Sint Lucas Antwerp.

I have a strong interest in mathematics, geometry, and technology. These themes often reappear throughout my work and serve as important sources of inspiration.

I am fascinated by the structures, patterns, and logic that underlie mathematical principles. Through my practice, I explore ways of translating these concepts into tangible objects and visual forms, making the beauty of mathematics visible through an artistic lens. By combining craftsmanship, research, and experimentation, I create work that sits at the intersection of art, design, and mathematics.

You can contact me via instagram bente.sieraad and via email bente.govaerts@gmail.com.
